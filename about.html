About Tesla Optimus Gen2

Concept & Vision

Optimus Gen2 is Tesla's humanoid robot designed to handle tasks unsuited for humans - whether they're dangerous, repetitive, or simply boring. Our vision is to create a general-purpose, bi-pedal, autonomous humanoid robot capable of performing unsafe, repetitive, or boring tasks.

The Optimus program represents Tesla's commitment to developing the next generation of automation, one that can navigate human environments and assist in ways previously only possible in science fiction. By leveraging our expertise in AI, manufacturing, and battery technology, we're creating a robot that's not just functional, but also economically viable for widespread adoption.

Key Milestones

2021

Announced during Tesla AI Day

Tesla first revealed the Optimus concept during the inaugural AI Day event, showcasing the company's vision for a general-purpose humanoid robot.

2022

First working prototype unveiled

The first functional Optimus prototype demonstrated basic mobility and object manipulation capabilities at Tesla's second AI Day event.

2023

Enhanced mobility and dexterity

Significant improvements in movement fluidity, balance control, and hand dexterity were achieved through iterative hardware and software development.

2024

Introduction of Gen2 model

The second generation Optimus debuts with dramatically improved performance, featuring enhanced AI capabilities, longer battery life, and more sophisticated sensor integration.
