Mechanical Structure

Optimus Gen2 features a lightweight yet durable frame constructed from advanced composite materials. The skeletal structure is designed to mimic human biomechanics while optimizing for strength-to-weight ratio.

The robot's joints utilize Tesla's proprietary actuator technology, which combines high torque output with precise position control. Each actuator contains integrated sensors that provide real-time feedback on position, velocity, and applied force.

Key Mechanical Features:

  • Carbon fiber composite endoskeleton
  • Titanium alloy joint components
  • Shock-absorbing structures in feet and spine
  • Modular limb design for easy maintenance
  • IP54 rated for dust and water resistance
