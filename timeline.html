Development Timeline

2021

Announcement at AI Day

Tesla officially reveals the Optimus project, highlighting the future of humanoid robots during the AI Day event.

2022

First Working Prototype

Tesla showcases a functional prototype demonstrating basic movements and AI-driven responsiveness.

2023

Advanced Iteration

Further improvements in actuator design, battery technology, and software integration set the stage for expanded pilot programs.

2024 & Beyond

Scaled Production

Tesla aims to begin scaled manufacturing of Optimus for industrial and domestic usage, pushing the boundaries of human-robot collaboration.
